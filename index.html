The Problem Statement

Each of us did at least 2 personal interviews to collect data but we also sent a survey out for more quantitative data. Here we prioritized our information and thought of a user flow for our "typical" user persona. With that, we split up the different parts of the user flow between the four of us to start making sketches of our own ideas of what the app would look like. We took bits and pieces from everyones sketches to start making our wireframes. What was the problem? What was our solution?
